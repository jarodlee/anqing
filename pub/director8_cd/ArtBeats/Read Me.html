Welcome...

Artbeats is pleased to include these clips from various Artbeats Digital Film Library titles for your use and enjoyment. This is only a small sampling of the large volume of imagery available. Please visit our website to see a broader overview of what we have to offer.

Artbeats--creator of the Artbeats Digital Film Library and many collections of still imagery--is a leading source for royalty-free content designed for use with digital editing & compositing, desktop video, multimedia, internet content creation and 3D animation and rendering. To provide the highest possible quality, most footage is sourced from 35mm film. The clips are then processed by Artbeats for color correction, dust & scratch removal, and--where applicable--looping, tiling, and alpha channel creation.

Subjects include pyrotechnic effects, water, backgrounds, clouds, archival footage, surface maps and much more. Frame sizes range from D1 NTSC or PAL resolutions through 2K (2048x1536) film resolution. All products are available on CD-ROM for multiple platforms.
